## Hardware-Ready Carry-Forward Paragraph

Phase 3 should preserve continuity with the existing hardware path by keeping the perturbation family local and Pauli-based, but it must shift the measurement language from state-return overlap toward a fixed-observable correlator.

The safe continuity rule is:

- keep the perturbation generator local (`X_q`-family) and support-declared
- keep the observable fixed in advance rather than sampling over an observable family
- prefer a hardware estimator phrased as a fixed Pauli or fixed subset-parity correlator
- preserve the same depth/trial/backend bookkeeping discipline already used by the q14 and q80 hardware artifacts

That is enough to call the path hardware-ready in scope, but not enough to say the hardware estimator is already solved.
